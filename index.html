About

I’m Aryan Singh, a filmmaker and writer based in Mumbai, India. Currently, I’m completing post production on my directorial feature debut, a film that continues my love for exploring how humour and human drama intersect with the world around us. My journey so far has taken me through writing and directing the independent feature Meri Jaan Bacha Meri Jaan, serving as an Assistant Director on Weekdays & Weekends (now streaming on Apple TV), and collaborating as Assistant/Co-Writer with Abhijeet Deshpande and Sai Kabir on projects including the upcoming feature Park Street.

Beyond films, I’ve hosted one-on-one conversations with filmmakers for Whitebirdtrails, which has given me fresh perspectives on the craft I love and helped me discover my voice. As of 2025, I’ve written the story of the feature film Chingam, officially selected at the Chicago South Asian Film Festival, and I’m currently writing and developing multiple projects at Jumping Tomato Studios under the direction of Rohandeep Singh Bisht. For me, every project is an experiment, a chance to play with form, stretch narrative, and discover new ways to connect with an audience.
